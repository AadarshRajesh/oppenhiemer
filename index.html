J. Robert Oppenheimer

About

During World War II, Lt. Gen. Leslie Groves Jr. he was appointed to work on the top-secret Manhattan Project.
He and a team of scientists spend years developing and designing the atomic bomb.
Their work comes to fruition on July 16, 1945, as the world witness the first nuclear explosion, forever changing the course of history
